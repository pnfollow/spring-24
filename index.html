Nebulae

Nebulae are commonly known as the nurseries of new stars. The main classifications of a nebulae are emmision, supernova remnant, planetary, reflection, and dark.

Galaxies

Galaxies are star systems with black holes at the center. The three main classifications of galaxies are spiral galaxies, elliptical galaxies, and irregular galaxies.
